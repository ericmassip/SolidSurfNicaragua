VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

We believe you shouldn’t have to choose between a surf vacation and a volunteer trip. We believe that you can volunteer while also taking the time to relax and recharge and believe that the best way to connect with a community is to immerse yourself within it by also lending a hand. That is why we have created a volunteer program that provides the flexibility to also surf, explore and relax while traveling in Nicaragua.

Prior to your stay, we work with two local non-profits and the El Transito community to plan small projects and/or volunteer sessions you can partake in. There are unlimited ways to make a difference and we cater your volunteer experience based on how you would like to help the community. Check out the video for more information and choose your project below!

ACTIVITY LIST

PHYSICAL EDUCATION
Kids Surf Class / Possible Sat 10am, 60min
Kids Yoga / Possible Mon-Sat 2pm-5pm, 30min
Women's Yoga / Possible Sun 5pm, 50min
Help us teach surfing and yoga to local kids (or adults) in order to encourage them to try something new and help build their confidence outside of just the classroom.

ENGLISH TEACHING ASSISTANT
Thursday / 1:30 pm - 2:30 pm (School)
Help facilitate instruction during English class by organizing students, assisting in delivering instruction and helping students complete classwork.

SERVE LUNCH TO SCHOOL KIDS
Weekdays / 9:30 am - 10:30 am (Elementary School)
Weekdays / 11:30 - 1:00 PM (High School)
NICA Fund encourages parents to send their children to school by serving breakfast for the primary school and lunch for the secondary school. Help to organize students, prepare plates and serve food.

TOWN CLEANUP
Weekdays 6:00 am - 7:00 am (Beach)
Weekdays 3:00 pm - 4:00 pm (Street)
Help keep our town clean through beach, water well, estuary and road cleanups, education on why throwing trash away and recycling is important.

PRE-K TEACHERS ASSISTANT
Weekdays / 9:00 am - 11:00 am
Great with kids? Then you can help the Pre-K teacher with instruction and classroom management. The kids are always eager to see new faces they can learn from.

CREA PROGRAM
Weekdays / 9:00am - 10:00 am
Weekdays / 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm
The CREA Program (Crea means to grow) uses iPads and computers to help increase children’s reading and math a bilities since most kids graduating High School only have a 7th grade reading level. Reflex Math is used for the younger kids and Khan Academy’s online program for older kids. You can assist in the classroom or donate to the classroom..

LEARNING GARDEN
Weekdays / 2:00 pm - 3:00 pm
Have a green thumb? Then help teach the local kids how to grow vegetables as well as tend the garden (water, plant and clean).

LOCAL SCHOOL PAINTING/BUILDING
Schedule Varies
Help us improve the structure of the school and provide materials to keep kids interested in their education and future and increase attendance. (Project available based on needs)

MEDICAL CLINIC
Monday - Thursday / 9:00 am - 11:00 am
Are you a nurse, doctor or dentist? Help us educate the town and aid in the development of the medical services available. Prior notice necessary for gainign health official approvals.

ART CENTER ACTIVITIES
Monday - Saturday / 2:00 pm - 5:00 pm
Teach arts and crafts to kids or do recycling/upcycling projects with kids and teach about environmenttal effects of waste(plastic in ocean, etc.)

TURTLE SANCTUARY
Possible September - November
Help protecting mother and baby turtles and witness the hatching process.
