Surf Breaks

SOLID is in front of four of the most consistent and un-crowded waves in Nicaragua. The bigger swell season is March - October (chest to overhead+) with fun sized waves from November - February (waist to head high).

Main Beach (Beginner+ / Right & Left)

Main beach is friendly, consistent and breaks from 1ft - 5ft. Your groveler or hybrid will work great and you can expect long lines and some air sections.

Pangas (Intermediate+ / Right & Left)

Directly to the left of main beach, this break is a bit steeper and heavier. Ride a standard shortboard or hybrid. Works 3ft - 6 ft with barrels/long lines.

The Corner (Beginner+/ Left)

At the left corner of the beach is a break that has a soft take off and can give you some of the longest rides in town. This break needs a little more swell to break.

Pistols (Advanced+ / Right & Left)

A quick steep takeoff great for advanced surfers looking to step up their game and learn to get barreled. Standard shortboard is recommended.

Miramar (Advanced+ / Left)

When home shuts downs (6ft+) travel north 25 min. for a reef point break. Quick barrel from the take off to a mellow line. Standard shortboard, possibly step-up.
