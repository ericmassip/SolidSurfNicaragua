Address/Phone in Nicaragua

100 mtrs west of Baseball Stadium
El Transito, Nagarote
+505 2350 4781

Tourist Visa: $10 Cash (entry only)

You’ll need to buy a 90-day tourist visa upon arrival.

Baggage Claim

Keep your ticket stub and passport readily available as there will be someone to confirm that you are taking the correct baggage.

Transportation / Pick Up

All-Inclusive Guests
Your driver will be waiting at the airport with either a SOLID T-shirt, a name sign or a SOLID “S” paddle. Please walk past the people towards the bar (on the right) to meet your driver. Call +505 2350 4781.

Bed + Breakfast / Surf Hostel Guests
Most taxi drivers know El Transito and SOLID. The rate is $60-$80 for the 1.5 - 2 hour ride.

Currency / ATM

Only cash is taken on site at SOLID.
Dollars are accepted everywhere so no need to exchange. However, make sure bills are not torn and it is recommended to have lower bill amounts. Also, there are no ATM’s in town.

Medical / Travel Insurance

We recommend purchasing travel insurance.
Please alert the staff of any conditions that we should be aware of and make sure to bring any necessary medications. There is a local clinic but the nearest hospital is 90 minutes away.

Tipping

It has been customary for each group to tip 5-10% on the trip, though it varies based on group size, length of stay and personal experience/preference.
