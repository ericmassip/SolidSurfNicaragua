TOUR ACTIVITIES

All-inclusive packages include transportation and guide to one of the tour activities listed below. The cost listed is what is estimated for the activity shall you choose to partake in as well as $20 - 25 for a city meal. Souvenirs in the city markets run from $5 - $50 from little bracelets, tank tops, blankets and trinkets to beautiful paintings, sculptures and hammocks.

Please sign up for the tour activity of your choice, and if you would like to add an additional tour to your package, the charge is $40 - $100 per person for transport / guide depending on the activity (based on length of tour and number of participants).

LOCAL ACTIVITIES

On non-tour days, we have a number of other activities to partake in. One surf lesson and two yoga classes are included in the all-inclusive package.
