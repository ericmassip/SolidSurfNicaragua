Explore Nicaragua

CITIES, SITES & ATTRACTIONS

Below lists the main cities and their attractions but make sure to explore the interactive map for all activities, sites, hotels/hostels, museams and activites SOLID recommendeds visiting and/or traveling to while in Nicaragua.

LEÓN AREA

Visit the city of Leon where you can check out the Cathedral, central square, History Museam, Museam of Myths and Legends, partake in Salsa dancing (Thursdays at El Olla Quemada), visit one of the only surf shops in the country (Get Up Stand Up) and get some good eats!

NEARBY TOURS/ACTIVITIES:

  • Rum Factory Tour
  • Sandboarding at Cerro Negro Volcano

GRANADA / MASAYA AREA

Visit the city of Granada where you can check out the Cathedral, central square, Art Gallery, Chocolate Museam, a cafe that has the largest hammock in Nicaragua (Cafe de las Sonrisas), enjoy a wonderful meal or get a massage (Mansion de Chocolate).

NEARBY TOURS/ACTIVITIES:

  • Mombacho Volcano - Zip lining, hiking
  • Masaya Volcano - Visit at night to see the red lava!
  • Masaya Market - Purchase from one of the largest local markets

ESTELI / MATAGALPA AREA

Pass through city of Esteli stopping at Pullasos Ole for a great steak and Maracuya dessert and check out the town center. Continue on up to Matagalpa where you can stay at a quaint bed & breakfast and have a night out in a great city that escapes the heat. In the morning do a chocolate tour at El Castilo de Chocolate and/or venture up to Selva Negra for some amazing local cheese or to get a tour of their organic farm or coffee tour.

NEARBY TOURS/ACTIVITIES:

  • Chocolate Tour at El Castillo
  • Organic Farm & Cheese Tour at Selva Negra
  • Coffee Tour at Selva Negra

SAN JUAN DEL SUR AREA

While San Juan del Sur is known for surfing, the actual town is on a bay and you’ll need a truck or boat to travel to the different surf breaks (Maderas and Playa Hermosa are two options). There are plenty of restaurants and this is where you can partake in a lively night life. If you’re looking to party, don’t miss out on the very popular Sunday Funday Bar/Hostel crawl. If mellow is more your seen then check out the San Juan del Sur Brewery.

NEARBY TOURS/ACTIVITIES:

  • Sunday Funday Bar Crawl
  • San Juan del Sur Cerveceria
  • Playa Maderas or Playa Hermosa for Surfing
