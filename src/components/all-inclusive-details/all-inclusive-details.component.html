WELCOME TO SOLID SURF & ADVENTURE

Thank you for choosing SOLID as we are very much looking forward to the opportunity of hosting you and your family/friends in Nicaragua! In order to ensure a wonderful stay at SOLID, we have pulled together information that will not only help you plan for your arrival, but also how to make the most of our services.

Our all-inclusive packages are created to eliminate the stress of planning and decision making, enabling our clients to have a fun and relaxing stay. While there are a few consistent parts to the daily schedule (breakfast @ 8:30 “ish”, lunch @ 1, dinner @ 6:30) we try to stay as flexible as possible, letting you choose when you want to surf, read, relax, do yoga, have a massage or take an adventure.

We encourage you to be proactive during your stay by informing your guide on what activities you would like to partake in (or submit your activity list via this app) so we can plan your week in the best way possible. Also, if you need some ideas or activity suggestions, please ask!

Furthermore, we welcome you to get to know our friendly local staff and encourage you to experience the culture through our volunteer opportunities.

Sincerely,
The SOLID Team

What to Expect

Meal Schedule & Dietary Preferences
We serve a weekly healthy menu of locally sourced and fresh fish, chicken, pork, shrimp, vegetables and fruit. If you have any dietary restrictions please note in the waiver and remind our guide. (Breakfast by 9:30, Lunch near 1pm, dinner around 6:30pm)

Water (Shower & Drinking)
Water tanks are refilled every other day so if there is a lack of water, please notify the staff to switch the tank. For drinking, please use the bottled water provided but you can use the sink for brushing your teeth.

Air Conditioning / Electricity
Please turn off the A/C while not in the room in order to save energy and ensure your room cools at night (daytime electric levels are sometimes low, so if the A/C doesn’t seem to be cooling it is most likely the electric). If the A/C does not work at night, please let us know!

Housekeeping
We do our best to freshen up rooms daily, but if you need an extra sweep or towel, just ask and we would be more than happy to do so!

Surfing
Most of the time the best surf will be right in front of SOLID but we do access other breaks (conditions permitting - skill level or swell/wind). Please keep in mind that we usually don’t need to travel to other locations because our break is the most consistent with less crowds and other spots only break with bigger swells. (This is also why camps at other locations need to travel.)

Note For Consideration

We thank you in advance for understanding that you are traveling to a third-world country where flexibility and patience is sometimes necessary (dirt roads, power outages, glitchy WiFi). Shall anything un-planned happen, please know we always do our best to remedy any issues quickly in order to make your stay as wonderful as possible!
